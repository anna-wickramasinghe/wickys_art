My Works

Here are some projects I have done as commissioned works and my own inspired artworks.

Kashi Bhai

One of my friends suggested that I try drawing this portrait of "Kashi" who is a character in a Bollywood movie named "Bajirao Mastani" by Sanjay Leela Bhansali. Since this character was portrayed by one of my favorite actresses, Priyanka Chopra, and the role of Kashi in the movie was so affectionate and strong, I fell in love with her and decided to draw her. Specifically, I loved her costume in this portrait and put in extra effort to make it as realistic as I could.
Hats off to the friend who suggested me to make this art

Arctic Treasure

The reference photo for this graphite art was a photograph named 'Arctic Treasure,' captured by wildlife photographer Sergey Gorshkov of an arctic wolf. The eyes of this majestic creature inspired me to create an artwork of him/her.

Deepu

Deepika Padukoun, is one of my favorite actresses who won my heart with her debut appearing in the movie, 'Om Shanthi Om'. Specially I love how I completed her hair in this drawing

Cheoljong

This is a photo of a scene from the Korean drama 'Mr. Queen,' featuring the main lead, King Cheoljong, portrayed by Kim Jung Hyun.
Drawing his fan was a new experience for me, and I believe I completed it successfully.

Unknown

One of my friends suggested that I try drawing this particular character as a pencil art and this turned out to be my first-ever graphite art, and the positive outcome boosted my confidence to create more graphite artworks. This experience made me realize that I have the potential to produce amazing artwork with just a pencil. Although I don't know the identity of this anime or mangatoon character, this became my inspiration to pursue artistry. Again hats off to you friend.

Buddy

I nearly got scammed by the person who placed the order to draw this pet portrait. However, I really love the end product, and this was my first attempt at drawing a short-haired animal. Despite being unable to contact the person who made the order, I decided to keep the artwork as part of my personal collection, and I never regert drawing this buddy.

Commissioned work

A commissined portrait done by me for a client request.

Lucky

This graphite art represents one of my early stages of artistic exploration. At a glance, you can see that I haven't followed perfect or professional strokes on the paper. However, this turned out to be a significant stage in my practice as a beginner. The character I attempted to draw was 'Merida' from the Disney movie 'Brave,' but it ended up resembling more of a friend of mine called 'Lucky' rather than 'Merida'.

Excalibur

This is an artistic representation of a scene from the TV series named 'Merlin.' It resembles the Sword of King Arthur, 'Excalibur,' in the hands of the spirit of the lake, 'Avalon'.
I used mixed materials to create this artwork, which also represents one of my early stages as an artist.
